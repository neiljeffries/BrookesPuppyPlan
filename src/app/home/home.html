Puppy Milestones

calendar_today Weeks 1–2

Help your Yorkie adjust to their new home. Focus on bonding, establishing a feeding schedule (3–4 small meals to prevent low blood sugar), and starting house training.

 school Weeks 3–4

Begin basic commands like sit, stay, and come. Socialise your Yorkie with people and other pets — this breed can become over-protective without early exposure.

 directions_walk Months 2–3

Continue vaccinations, begin leash training with a lightweight harness (to protect their delicate trachea), and introduce short walks. Enrol in a puppy class.

 pets Months 4–6

Teething peaks — keep small chew toys available. Start a daily brushing routine for their silky coat and introduce dental care early. Work on consistent obedience and recall training.

Quick Tips

schedule Stick to a Schedule

Yorkies thrive on routine. Feed them 3–4 small meals a day, walk, and put them to bed at the same times each day to prevent stress and blood sugar drops.

 emoji_events Positive Reinforcement

Reward good behaviour with tiny treats and praise. Yorkies are sensitive — avoid punishment and harsh tones. Patience and gentle consistency go a long way with this breed.

 nightlight Let Them Rest

Yorkie puppies need 18–20 hours of sleep per day. Provide a warm, quiet space away from drafts — their small size means they lose body heat quickly.
